Mission statement

The State High LGBTA Alliance aims to promote full acceptance of all students, regardless of sexual orientation or gender identity, through a comprehensive range of public awareness activities, education, social action, and advocacy. The goal is to create and maintain a safe, welcoming, and inclusive environment by cultivating mutual respect, open discussion, and a common vision of social equality.
